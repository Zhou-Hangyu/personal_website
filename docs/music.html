Who doesn't love music?

Music = Time Capsule

I love music. You don’t need to become a singer or songwriter to just fall in love with music. Euphonious melodies bring happiness to everyone. I spend more than 1 hour a day listening to my favorite pieces, 5 hours a week discovering new works, and every once in a while, picking up an old song that I loved, sit back in the corner of my room, savoring memories of those past days.

To me, music is more than just lyrics and rhythm; they are time capsules -- each one of them contains a piece of memory that could take me back to those old days. There are a thousand Hamlets in a thousand people's eyes. Whenever we listen to music, we attach feelings and memories of that moment to them; we are creating our own songs, songs that contain not just words and melody but also the emotion, the scene -- the memory of that particular moment. And if we listen to them after months or years, it will just like opening a bottle of good wine; the strong flavor of the past will blow me away.
