Imagine you are in a society with highly advanced technology, where people could create virtual environments and live in there forever without returning to the real world. These virtual worlds have their own "reality," that is, the physical rules that determine the way every particle moves. Maybe in that world, gravity would push objects away, the time would flow back.

For some reason, the virtual worlds are built in a way that no one could prove the fakeness of it with its own rules. In other words, the world is real to people who have lived there for their whole life.

So how do you know if we are not living in a world like this?

How do you know if the reality we are taking for granted is the actual reality?

That’s a simple question -- we don’t know.
